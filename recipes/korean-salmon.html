Korean Salmon

Description

This is a quick and easy dish that takes you to Korea, with its striking flavour and vibrant colour.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons (45 g) gochujang sauce
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • Â½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon chopped scallion greens
  • 4 salmon fillets (roughly 450g in total)
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Set your oven to 220 degrees celcius.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the gochujang sauce, sesame oil, honey, rice wine vinegar and ginger.
  3. Put the salmon on a foil-lined or baking paper-lined baking tray. Coat the salmon in the glaze, and sprinkle lightly with salt.
  4. Broil 8-10 minutes, until the salmon looks opaque. It is up to you how 'done' you like your salmon. Having the oven at a high temperature will help caramelise the glaze on the salmon.
  5. To serve, sprinkle with the scallion and the toasted sesame seeds if you have them.
